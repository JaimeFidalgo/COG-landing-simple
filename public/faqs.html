PROJECT

What is the Chans of Glory project all about?

Chains of Glory is a community driven, NFTs + DeFi videogame in the Metaverse. It intends to create a strong community by providing users with a great gaming experience within a sustainable economic ecosystem based on the native platform token COG. We have called that Play 2 Enjoy & Earn.

What is Blockchain Technology?

A Blockchain is a data structure that solves the double-spending problem with no need of reliable intermediaries. Thus, providing a trustworthy environment to transfer value peer-to-peer.

Chains of Glory benefits from Blockchain intrinsic characteristics, inmutability and transparency, by publicly storing in-game users information. That way, users are real owners of their characters, turning them into digital assets that can be purchased and sold.

What is a Smart Contract?

A Smart Contract is an open source piece of code that once deployed into a Blockchain Network becomes inmutable. Thus, regulating interactions between various parties in a transparent and reliable way without the need of intermediaries.

Chains of Glory benefits from Smart Contracts since the entire back-end of the game is coded under that paradigm. That way, code becomes law and users can be totally sure that what is coded is what is going happen.

What is the COG Token used for?

COG is the native token of the platform and its most relevant uses go as follows:

  • Engagement: COG will grant voting rights to in-game proposals made by community and founders.
  • Minting: COG will be user-minted after the completion of quests.
  • Purchase: COG will be used for in-game equipment acquisitions.
  • DeFi: COG reserves will be reinvested in DeFi protocols. Benefits will revert to the community.
  • Incentivize: COG will be rewarded to those users that benefit the community.
  • Burn: COG will be regularly burned to mantain the ecosystemâ€™s health.
What is an NFT?

A non-fungible token is a non-interchangeable unit of data stored on a blockchain, a form of digital ledger, that can be sold and traded.

Chains of Glory benefits from NFTs since every character, equipment and user in-game status are stored on the Blockchain Network in the form of NFTs. Thus, giving control to the players over their digital assets.

What is DeFi?

Decentralized finance offers financial instruments without relying on intermediaries such as brokerages, exchanges, or banks by using smart contracts on a blockchain.

Chains of Glory benefits from DeFi since it allows to generate revenue that does not rely on new players coming into the platform. Thus, fostering a sustainable in-game economic system.

What is the Metaverse?

The Metaverse is an interoperable, composable and open ecosystem where users, protocols and companies interact between each other.

Chains of Glory benefits from the Metaverse since the entire game architecture is designed to scale with no limits within this new ecosystem.

What is Play to Enjoy and Earn and how it differs from Play to Earn?

Our team's goal was in first instance to develop a fun game that did not follow a Ponzi Scheme system. When you play Chains Of Glory, your gaming experience and potential earnings are not dependent on a ceaseless flow of new players.
