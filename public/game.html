Game Lore

the continent of praedium is being devastated by the hysteria, a terrible plague that turns every living creature to madness.

the kingdoms of krimah, khal, and nostro have to face the plague if they do not want to confront a chaotic future.

the delirium island is the only place on earth where the chains of glory plant grows. it's magical characteristics will play an important role in Praedium's destiny...
