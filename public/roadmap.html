  1. Alpha Version

    Jul 1st, 2022

    Alpha Version will be released for development purposes. Deployed on a testnet Network. Targeting 1K users.

    November 1st, 2022

    First Private Round for Investors to buy the COG Token

  3. Open Beta

    October 1st, 2022

    Open Beta will serve to improve game features. Deployed on mainnet Network. Targeting 5K users.

  4. Private Sale 2

    October 15th, 2022

    Second Private Round for Investors to buy the COG Token.

  5. Public Sale

    November 15th, 2022

    Public Round for Users to buy the COG Token.

    January 1st, 2023
