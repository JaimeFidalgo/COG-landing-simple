ALPHA SEASONS

Alpha Seasons are 1-month periods where you will have access to the main functionalities of the game in the Metis Stardust Testnet Network.
During these periods, every advancement within the gameplay will be lost at the end of each Season.
However, by that time, the players that advance most in the game and those who provide more feedback in the Discord Server will be rewarded.
To participate in the Alpha Season, just follow the steps below:

STEP 1

  Join our Discord Community

STEP 2

  Follow us on Twitter

STEP 3

  Fill in the Alpha Season Registration Form
